
car license plate detection - v1 2022-05-12 4:23pm
==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on May 12, 2022 at 10:59 AM GMT

It includes 1800 images.
License-plate are annotated in YOLO v5 PyTorch format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 720x480 (Stretch)

No image augmentation techniques were applied.
